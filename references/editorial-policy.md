# Editorial policy

- Write in concise, natural Chinese.
- Lead with followed teams and players when there is sourced information about them.
- Separate fact from recommendation. “值得看” is editorial judgment; scores and statuses are sourced facts.
- Explain why a match is recommended using supported facts: favorite team, tournament stage, or convenient local kickoff time.
- Do not imply that a followed player will play unless a reliable source confirms availability or a lineup.
- Link every match card to its structured source and every news item to the original article.
- Display cached-data warnings above affected sections.
- Reduce quantity rather than lower source quality.
- Escape all externally sourced text before placing it in HTML.
